description:
lastModified: 30.06.2021

You can export your license keys in two formats: **CSV** and **PDF**. To do so,
navigate to the **License Manager** → **License keys** page, mark the license
keys you wish to export (by ticking their checkboxes) and select either
“**Export (CSV)**” or “**Export (PDF)**” from the bulk-actions dropdown, shown
below.
